## 5. Enquiry Response Email

**Subject:** Your PT Enquiry — Next Steps

Hi {{client_name}},

Thanks for getting in touch — I've received your enquiry and I'll be following up shortly.

Here's what happens next:

**Step 1 — Consultation call**
I'll reach out within 24 hours to arrange a free 15–30 minute call. This is where we talk through your goals, current fitness level, schedule, and whether we're a good fit.

**Step 2 — PAR-Q & Health Screening**
Before we start any sessions, you'll need to complete a PAR-Q (Physical Activity Readiness Questionnaire) and a short health screening form. This is standard practice and ensures I programme sessions that are safe and appropriate for you specifically. I'll send this digitally — it takes around 5 minutes.

**Step 3 — First session**
Once screening is complete and we've agreed on a programme, we book your first session and get started.

A few things worth knowing upfront:
- Sessions are mobile — I come to you (home, park, or agreed outdoor location in {{service_area}})
- I require 24 hours' notice for cancellations — late cancellations are charged in full
- Payment is taken in advance per session or as a block booking

If you have any questions before our call, reply here and I'll get back to you same day.
